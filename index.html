Martin Luther King Jr.

Memorable civil rights fighter

A timeline of Martin Luther King Jr.'s life:

  • 1929 - Martin Luther King Jr. is born on January 15 in Atlanta, Georgia.
  • 1948 - King graduates from Morehouse College with a Bachelor of Arts degree in sociology.
  • 1953 - King marries Coretta Scott and begins his career as a minister in Montgomery, Alabama.
  • 1955 - King leads the Montgomery Bus Boycott after Rosa Parks is arrested for refusing to give up her seat on a bus.
  • 1963 - King delivers his famous "I Have a Dream" speech at the March on Washington for Jobs and Freedom.
  • 1964 - King is awarded the Nobel Peace Prize.
  • 1968 - King is assassinated on April 4 in Memphis, Tennessee.
